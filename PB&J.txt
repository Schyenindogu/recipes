Ingredients:
  - Peanut Butter (crunchy preferred)
  - Jelly
  - 2 slices of bread

Tools:
  - Knife (not too sharp)
  - a plate to put stuff on

Directions:
  1. Take knife and spread peanut butter on one slice. Set slice aside.
  2. Take knife and spread jelly on the other slice.
  3. Take both slices and gently press together (jelly side and peanut butter side must meet.)
